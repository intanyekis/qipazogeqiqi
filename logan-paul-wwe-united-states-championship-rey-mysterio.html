Logan Paul visits pawn shop to see how much he could get for WWE belt - then gets asked out

June 13, 2024
blog

Logan Paul attempted to sell his WWE belt at a pawn shop and ended up being asked on a date.

The YouTuber-turned-wrestling star is the WWE United States Champion having won the title at the Crown Jewell event in Saudi Arabia.

It was there that he beat wrestling legend Rey Mysterio by using a pair of brass knuckles to eventually pin the 48-year-old and claim the belt.

Over a week on and The Maverick is seemingly keen to offload his belt, offering a pawnbroker the United States title for $100,000.

Asked by the store owner if he was allowed to buy it, Paul replied: "if you have a good offer".

The pawnbroker then asked who would purchase the belt for such a price, Paul said: "I don't know the history behind this belt, but I do know it was taken off Rey Mysterio.
