Jodi Lyn O’Keefe is an American actress, fashion designer, and model who is best known for portraying roles such as Gretchen Morgan in Prison Break, Jo Laughlin in The Vampire Diaries, Cassidy Bridges in Nash Bridges, and Lionel Davenport in Hit the Floor. She has also appeared in a variety of other TV shows and films such as Halloween H20, She’s All That, The Frozen Ground, Whatever It Takes, Dharma & Greg, The Help, Tru Calling, Two and a Half Men, Charmed, Boston Legal, Criminal Minds, CSI: NY, The Big Bang Theory, Law & Order: LA, Lost, Lucifer, Legacies, and many more. Jodi has done voiceover work in video games for characters such as Kelly Weaver in Command & Conquer: Red Alert 3: Uprising.
