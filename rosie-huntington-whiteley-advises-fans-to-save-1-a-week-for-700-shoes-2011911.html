Rosie Huntington-Whiteley Tells Fans to Save $1 a Week for $700 Shoes

August 21, 2024
blog

Rosie Huntington-Whiteley's advice to feel like a sexy woman will take 14 years to achieve.

At last week's launch party for Christian Louboutin's new book at Barney's in Beverly Hills, the Transformers actress and Victoria's Secret model told Fox411's Pop Tarts column what she likes about wearing the famous red-soled heels.

"They make me feel like a woman. I feel they make you look very sexy, the cuts are beautiful, the lines, the colors, the embellishments," revealed the 24-year-old British beauty. "I think [Christian Louboutin] managed to capture something special for women, every girl should be lucky enough to have them."

But for prices starting around $700, it's not so easy for every girl to get lucky enough. Huntington-Whiteley offered advice for girls to feel as sexy and feminine as she does. "If you can save up for a pair put away a dollar a week. It's worth it girls. It is really worth it!" she explained.
