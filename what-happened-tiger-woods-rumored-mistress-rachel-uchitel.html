What Happened To Rachel Uchitel?

According to Page Six, Rachel is gearing up to release an explosive new book with agent David Vigliano – who has previously worked with a list of famous names like Michael Jackson, Janet Jackson, Britney Spears, and Justin Timberlake.

The outlet reported that the book will document the former mistress’ whole life story, including her high-profile affair with Tiger Woods.

It can be remembered that a story was published in November 2009 claiming that the golfer had had an extramarital affair with Rachel, who was a New York City nightclub manager at the time and who denied the relationship.

Now, people may finally hear her side of the story.

Rachel Has Been Sharing Details About Her Time With Tiger

The source claimed, “Everyone thinks they know her story, but they really don’t.” Before rising to fame and being known as Tiger Woods’ mistress, Rachel worked as a correspondent for Extra, opened a toy store, and became a spokesperson for sugar daddy site Seeking Arrangements.
