Who Is Rui Hachimura? - Rui Hachimura Wikipedia

Rui Hachimura Wikipedia: - Rui Hachimura is a Japanese professional basketball player. He was born in Toyama, Japan, on February 8, 1998. He is a player with the National Basketball Association's (NBA) Los Angeles Lakers. He has represented Japan on the international basketball scene and played collegiate basketball for the Gonzaga Bulldogs.

He fills the roles of power forward and small forward. He was regarded by ESPN as one of the top foreign players joining college when he signed a National Letter of Intent to play college basketball for the Gonzaga Bulldogs in the United States on November 21, 2015. On November 11, 2016, Hachimura made his Gonzaga Bulldogs regular season debut against Utah Valley, tallying one point and three rebounds in four minutes.

The Washington Wizards selected him ninth overall in the 2019 NBA draft, and in 2020, he was included on the NBA All-Rookie Second Team. Hachimura was dealt to the Los Angeles Lakers on January 23, 2023, along with three second-round draft selections and Kendrick Nunn.
