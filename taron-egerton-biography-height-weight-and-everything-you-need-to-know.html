Taron Egerton Biography, Height, Weight, And Everything You Need To Know

Taron Egerton is a Welsh actor known for his roles in movies such as “Kingsman: The Secret Service” and its sequel, “Rocketman,” and “Eddie the Eagle.” Here’s a brief biography and information about his height, weight, and other details:

Taron Egerton Biography

Taron David Egerton was born on November 10, 1989, in Birkenhead, Merseyside, England. He grew up in Aberystwyth, Wales, and later attended the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art (RADA) in London, where he honed his acting skills. Egerton began his acting career on stage before transitioning to television and film.

He gained widespread recognition for his portrayal of Gary “Eggsy” Unwin in the action-comedy film “Kingsman: The Secret Service” (2014) and its sequel, “Kingsman: The Golden Circle” (2017), directed by Matthew Vaughn. His performance in “Rocketman” (2019), a musical biopic about Elton John, further solidified his reputation as a talented actor. Egerton received critical acclaim for his role in “Rocketman” and won a Golden Globe Award for Best Actor in a Motion Picture – Musical or Comedy.

Taron Egerton Height and Weight

Taron Egerton stands approximately 5 feet 9 inches tall (175 cm) and weighs around 165 pounds (75 kg). Please note that these measurements are approximate and may vary slightly.

Taron Egerton Other Notable Works

Apart from the aforementioned films, Taron Egerton has appeared in several other notable projects. Some of his other films include “Legend” (2015), “Eddie the Eagle” (2016), “Robin Hood” (2018), and “The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance” (2019) TV series, where he provided the voice for the lead character Rian.

Egerton’s talent extends beyond acting, as he has showcased his singing abilities in the biographical musical film “Rocketman” and the animated film “Sing” (2016), where he voiced the character of Johnny and performed several songs.

Overall, Taron Egerton has established himself as a versatile actor capable of portraying diverse roles on screen.
