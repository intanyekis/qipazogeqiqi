How Close Are Michael J. Fox And Christopher Lloyd In Real Life?

June 15, 2024
blog

The Back to the Future trilogy cast-mates are best friends in real life. Michael J. Fox and Christopher Lloyd recently had an emotional reunion at the New York Comic-Con.

Their lifelong friendship started unexpectedly on the sets of the classic sci-fi film franchise Back to the Future. The veteran actors met again on stage at the New York Comic-Con in October 2022. The videos from this impromptu emotional meeting have gone viral and garnered over 7 million views on social media. Reminiscing about their passion for films and going back in time, the legendary actors enthralled the audience with anecdotes from the set of sci-fi/comedy films. Let us look at their wonderful friendship that has stood the test of time.

Michael J. Fox replaced actor Eric Stoltz as Marty Mcfly in Back to the Future after the production had already shot a few scenes. Christopher Lloyd wasn't so happy about the re-shoot back then, but it's safe to say he's happy with Michael J. Fox joining production.

8 Michael J. Fox And Christopher Lloyd Were Complete Strangers At The Beginning

Michael J. Fox and Christopher Lloyd's story of friendship had an unusual beginning. During the shoot of the first installment of the film franchise Director Robert Zemeckis realized actor Eric Stoltz wasn't fit for the role of a slacker teenager named Marty Mcfly. Production was already six weeks in, so there would be additional costs to re-shoot the scenes with a new actor. The director wanted someone with comic flare; hence Michael J. Fox was recommended for the role.

He had been busy filming the Family Ties television series. The five-time Emmy-winning actor was apprehensive at the beginning about taking up a part in a sci-fi film. On the other hand, Christopher Lloyd wasn’t too happy about the changes. He got wary that they would have to re-shoot the changes with a new actor. When the two finally met on the sets, there was instant chemistry, and the rest, as you know, is history.

7 Michael J. Fox And Christopher Lloyd’s Friendship Began On The Sets Of Back To The Future 3

Michael J. Fox and Christopher Lloyd's friendship truly blossomed on the sets of Back to the Future 3. Before that, they were just mutual admirers. The two veteran actors became close friends only after filming two installments of the sci-fi franchise. On the sets of Back to the Future 3, they bonded over their passion for acting, books, and films.

During the recent New York Con stage event, Michael J. Fox described that he admired Christopher Llyod's acting skills and their lengthy discussions on Shakespeare’s "King Lear." He loved the fact that his best friend could explain scenes in the simplest of ways. Since acting was a priority for both, they guided each other in the right direction throughout the filming of the sci-fi/comedy franchise.

6 Christopher Lloyd's Emotional Support During Michael J. Fox's Parkinson’s Reveal

Michael J. Fox had been harboring a deep medical health secret while working on the sets of Spin City (1996-2000). He had been diagnosed with Parkinson’s in 1991 but was dreading to announce the same publically. He lived in a state of self-denial for seven years while carrying out his professional commitments dutifully.

During these seven years of soul-searching, he was under constant mental stress and had turned alcoholic. Christopher Lloyd stood by him like a good friend during the struggle and also when he was ready to go public about his medical condition. Since the inception of The Michael J. Fox Foundation For Parkinson's Research, Lloyd has been the biggest supporter of the medical cause. Recently during a heartwarming reunion at the New York Con, Michael thanked his best friend with a hug for standing beside him like a rock all these years.

5 Michael J. Fox And Christopher Lloyd Reunited On Stage After 36 Years

Fans got a treat of the talented duo coming together after almost 36 years for the Awesome Con in August 2021. Michael J. Fox posted a picture on Instagram with Christopher Lloyd, where they were enjoying quality time on a golf cart at the Con. They even asked fans to caption the funny moment. Their camaraderie was infectious, and even after decades, the friendship looked unbeatable. They like to keep their fans engaged with their social media banter.

4 Michael J. Fox And Christopher Lloyd Reunited Again For New York Comic Con

Michael J. Fox and Christopher Llyod met up again in October 2022 for the New York Comic Con panel discussion. There they spoke at length about their budding friendship on the sets of the trilogy and how it is still intact.

The Back to the Future panel discussion at the convention was packed with fans of the franchise. The famous duo exhibited the same camaraderie and chemistry even after years, for which they are legendary. The video from the emotional reunion has since garnered over 7 million views on social media.

3 Michael J. Fox Showed His Affection For Christopher Lloyd Through Social Media

"We were both tested and cleared to be together today" - this was the emotional message posted by Michael J. Fox for his lifelong friend, Christopher Lloyd, on the latter's birthday.

The Honorary Oscar Award recipient took to Instagram with this sweet post and wished his friend a healthy future. Referring to Lolyd as a brother, Michael captured the essence of how their friendship has stood the test of time.

2 Did Michael J. Fox And Christopher Lloyd Keep In Touch?

The skilled actors may occasionally make a few appearances on the stage for the media and fans, but in their personal lives, the two are constantly in touch. They enjoy poker nights and virtual reunions with their cast mates even today.

The Back to the Future cast had a virtual reunion after 35 years in May 2020. The crew re-enacted some of the iconic scenes from the film franchise for the YouTube series Reunited Apart. The video has garnered over 3.1 million views on the streaming platform since then.
