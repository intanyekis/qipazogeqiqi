Otto Wood is an American vocalist and drummer who is known for being the drummer and backing vocalist of Waterparks, a pop-rock band formed in Houston, Texas in 2011. With the band, he has released a number of tracks, albums, and extended plays which include Double Dare, Fandom, Black Light, Cluster, Easy to Hate, Beating Heart Baby, High Definition, Lowkey as Hell, Blonde, Watch What Happens Next, and Crave.

    Otto Wood Facts

  • He grew up on a farm in Houston, Texas.
  • His fellow Waterparks bandmates include lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist Awsten Knight and backing vocalist and lead guitarist Geoff Wigington.
  • Otto Wood joined Waterparks in 2012.
  • Along with his Waterparks bandmate Awsten Knight, he was previously a member of some other band.
  • With Waterparks, Otto Wood released the band’s debut studio album, Double Dare, on November 4, 2016, via Equal Vision. It included tracks like Royal, Dizzy, Take Her to the Moon, Gloom Boys, It Follows, I’ll Always Be Around, Made in America, and Stupid for You. The song Stupid for You was also nominated for an Alternative Press Music Award in the “Song of the Year” category in 2017.
  • He released his 2nd studio album titled Entertainment with Waterparks on January 26, 2018, and it was ranked at number #6 on the UK Rock & Metal Albums (OCC) chart, at number #7 on the US Top Alternative Albums (Billboard), at number #13 on the US Top Rock Albums (Billboard) chart, and at number #14 on the UK Independent Albums (OCC) chart. Logan White of Substream Magazine stated, “Entertainment is fun, catchy, and even a little bit unpredictable. Waterparks have pushed forward to become the best version of themselves that they can be, proving that they are here to stay.”
