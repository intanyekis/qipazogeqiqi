Eva Marcille files for divorce from Michael Sterling

Eva Mercille and Michael Sterling are calling it quits.

The Real House of Atlanta star, 38, filed for divorce from the lawyer on March 23  citing that their marriage is “irretrievably broken.”

She said in a statement,

“This has been one of the hardest decisions I have ever had to make, but sometimes life takes your journey in a direction you were not expecting,”

“Our children remain our biggest priority and the eight and half years we have spent together will always be cherished. We ask that you respect our privacy at this time.”

Marcille’s attorney stated in the filing, “are currently living in a bona fide state of separation,” 

The couple who married in October 2018 share three children: son Michael Sterling Jr., whom they welcomed in 2018, and Maverick Sterling, 3.

Sterling also is the father to Eva’s daughter Marley Rae from her past relationship with her ex-boyfriend Kevin McCall.

The reality star seeks legal and primary custody of all their kids and child support. She also hopes to receive child support, care, maintenance, and medical costs.

The former America’s Next Top Model contestant also hopes the court awards her “equitable division” of the assets they gained during their years-long marriage.

Moreover, the Young & the Restless actress also wants their debt to be equally divided.
