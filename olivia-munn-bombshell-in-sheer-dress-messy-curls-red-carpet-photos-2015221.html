Olivia Munn Goes Total Bombshell in Sheer Dress, Curls on Red Carpet

August 08, 2024
blog

Bombshell alert! 

Olivia Munn stepped out to promote the release of her latest film, Mortdecai, in Hollywood on Wednesday, Jan. 21, and she had no qualms about flaunting her assets on the red carpet!

The Newsroom actress, 34, often chooses to show just a hint of skin on the red carpet, but she really amped up the va-va-voom with her latest ensemble: a long-sleeved black dress by Ralph Rucci boasting a strategic, semi-sheer bodice that put her ample cleavage on display.

While Munn kept her makeup simple with a feminine palette of soft pink hues, the Magic Mike star, dating quarterback Aaron Rodgers since May 2014, went glam on her hair with mussed curls—a look hairstylist Christian Marc told Us Weekly was inspired by her dress. “Olivia is wearing a gorgeous dress that is very sexy, with a body-hugging silhouette,” Marc said. To complement the design, he decided on a “glamorous, mid-’70s style—very high fashion.”

To create the look, the celeb stylist worked Leonor Greyl Mousse au Lotus Volumatrice into Munn’s wet hair, making sure to evenly distribute the cream from her roots to her tips.

Once the actress’ hair was dry, Marc began to curl her brunette tresses using two different curling irons: a 1-inch tool for the back pieces and a 1.5-inch curler for the hair closest to her face, the combination creating a more relaxed style.

Before brushing out the curls for a lightly tousled look (using both a Mason Pearson brush and his fingers), Marc added some strong-hold Leonor Greyl Éclat Naturel styling cream, which helped to hold Munn’s curls in place throughout the premiere. He set the style with hairspray, and off she went!
