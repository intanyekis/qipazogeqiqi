Michele Morrone Career

Acting Career

Morrone’s acting career began in 2011 when he played Riccardo in Second Chance, a three-episode online series made by Piergiorgio Seidita that ran for one season and was then canceled.

After he got better at his job, he started taking the lead in short films like “Il Tempo di una Cigaretta” and “E La Vita Continua.”

Later, he was in the drama series Che Dio Ci Aiuti and several Italian TV shows, like Come Un Delfino (2011), Squadra Antimafia (2015), Provaci Ancora (2015), Sirene (2016), Renata Fonte (2017), Medici (2018), and Il Processo (The Trial) (2018).

In 2016, the 11th season of Dancing with the Stars Italia, he came in second (Ballando con le Stelle). In 2019, Morrone played the lead role of Luigi in the play Bar Giuseppe, and 2020 was his big year.

He became well-known after playing the lead role of a Mafia crime boss in the movie 365 Days. He then signed a three-year contract with the production company and appeared in two more sequels, 365 Days: This Day and The Next 365 Days.
