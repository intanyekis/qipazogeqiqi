How to Buy Quality Black Mid Calf Boots: A Buyer’s Guide

Mid calf boots are the perfect choice between ankle boots and knee-high boots, especially when it comes to style and all-around versatility. Plus, they are the best way to get around common fitting problems that often occur when you wear longer boots. 

To help you find the perfect pair of black mid calf boots, we have created this buying guide.

What to Look For

Price

Who doesn't want to buy a decent pair of shoes without making a hole in their pocket? Obviously, any shoe enthusiast would want to snag the best deal. Always keep your budget in mind and look for online marketplaces like Amazon to get amazing discounts.

Brand

To promote best buying practices, you need to do your research and find out the top brands for buying mid calf boots. This will give you an idea of both price and quality, and you might find better products at lower prices.

Reviews from previous buyers

Past customers often leave reviews about their experience and smart buyers read them. These reviews can help you decide if a product is fit for you or not. 

How to Measure Your Feet 

When it comes to measuring your feet for an online purchase, you need to take measurements of the following: 

Calf circumference

This is the fullest part of the boot, so you should measure around your calf.

Ankle circumference

To achieve this measurement, measure around the narrowest part of your ankle.

Shaft height

The shaft height is the most important. It is measured from the bottom of your foot to the top of the mid calf boot.

Heel height

The heel starts at the end of the boot sole.

How to Care for Mid Calf Boots 

If you live in a snowy or rainy area, you need to take special care of your boots. Snow can leave marks if not cleaned properly, and the situation can get worse on rainy days. Dirt and mud can also stain your black boots.

To keep your black mid calf boots clean, you should pick an easy to clean material. If you wear them daily, thoroughly clean the boots at least twice a week and keep them polished to keep stains at bay.

People Also Asked

Can I style mid calf boots with skinny jeans?

Mid calf boots and skinny jeans are the perfect combo if you want to create a laid-back, stylish outfit. Complete your look with a multi-colored floral biker jacket and you will be looking like a thousand bucks in no time!

Are black mid calf boots good for summer?

There is a misconception that black shoes heat up more, making them imperfect for the summer months. Mid calf boots can be worn all year round, and to optimize their style you just need to pick the material wisely.

Because of their versatility, you can easily incorporate them into all sorts of outfits.

How many inches is a decent-sized mid calf boot?

Your average pair of mid calf boots will measure at 9 inches to 13 ½ inches shaft, and most work boots fall in this range to balance protection and flexibility. If a boot has a shaft height of 13 ¾ inches to 17 ¾ inches, then you could consider this a taller boot.

Do mid calf boots look flattering?

If you want to achieve conventionally flattering proportions, then a low or mid-heel mid calf boot is good for you. If you’re unsure how mid calf boots will look on you, consider trying on a pair at your local mall. You don’t need to buy the pair you try on, and if you like how they fit you can go ahead and purchase the ones you saw online!

Will mid calf boots stain from water exposure?

Unfortunately, due to the material of mid calf boots, they are prone to staining. If yours are exposed to water, be sure to wipe them off with a dry cloth to prevent spotting.
