Lillian E. Lehman Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Lillian Lehman (born February 12, 1947) is an American actress. She played Lena Hart in NBC's soap opera Sunset Beach, and Dr. Joyce Meadows on ABC's General Hospital. She has also been cast in various guest roles on TV, as well as a starring role in Tenafly and Fay. Lehman is a graduate of California State University Northridge with a degree in theatre.She has also acted in films, such as Defending Your Life and Evan Almighty. A few of her TV credits include Magnum P.I., General Hospital, ER, Cold Case, one episode of Married... with Children and a recurring role as Judge Tiano on the TNT series The Closer.She has filmed hundreds of on-camera and voice-over commercials. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
