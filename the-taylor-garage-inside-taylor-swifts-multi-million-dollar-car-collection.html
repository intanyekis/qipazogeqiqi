Taylor Swift is a sensational Pop Star known for her singing, music composition, and acting. The 12-time Grammy Award winner has come a long way since her debut album was released in 2006. The singer has released ten studio albums and three re-recorded albums, with0 prestigious awards under her wing, such as the Grammy, VMA, Billboards Music Awards, and more. Being one of the top-selling artists globally, the 33-year-old’s estimated $740 million net worth is only predicted to shoot up.

The 'Midnight' singer’s net worth has allowed her to indulge in her passion for unique purchases like private jets and a versatile car collection. With luxury, comfort, and speed, the artist's multi-million-dollar garage has it all. Here are the top 8 cars from Taylor Swift’s exquisite automobile collection.

Chevrolet Silverado - $20,000

Taylor Swift was presented with her first truck, a pink 2007 Chevrolet Silverado, by her then-record label, Big Machine Records, as her 18th birthday gift. With a hefty curb weight of 4.797 pounds, it also features a powerful engine capable of producing 315 HP and 338 lb-ft torque. Reportedly, the singer donated the truck within a year to a children’s charity named Victory Junction Gang in 2008.

Nissan Qashqai - $25,000

The latest addition to Swift’s Collection is the low-key Nissan Qashqai. According to The U.S Sun, a 1.3-liter gasoline engine generating 148 HP, this humble purchase is the star’s new everyday vehicle. Besides, the height and reach of the adjustable steering wheel and well-assembled and stylish interiors with embroidered faux leather panels on the dashboard and doors make this car a blend of comfort, style, and practicality. Reportedly, the Grammy-winning singer added this compact SUV to her collection in 2022 to go out and about incognito in London with her then-boyfriend, Joe Alwyn.

Mercedes-Benz Viano - $30,000

A humble purchase, the Mercedes-Benz Viano is the proper mixture of style and practicality. A comfort-oriented SUV, the Viano’s best feature is the leather rotating seats so travelers can face each other. Ideal for on-the-road jam sessions, this car can seat up to 8 passengers and comes in various options ranging from diesel to gasoline, producing 136 HP and 258 HP, respectively. Taylor’s ownership of this modest car shows her appreciation for simple elegance and comfort.

Toyota Sequoia - $59,405

Continuing her admiration for big-comfort cars, the singer added an all-black Toyota Sequoia to her garage in 2014. An incredible mix of value and performance, the Sequoia is powered by a 5.8-liter V8 engine delivering 381 HP and 401 lb-ft of torque.

‘Swifties’ may know their Queen’s love for quick getaways and the need to hide from the paparazzi, making the Sequoia a great choice to blend well with the cars around.

Porsche 911 Turbo (997) S Coupe - $126,200

A favorite among many A-list celebrities and industrialists, the gorgeous Porsche 911 Turbo (997) S Coupe can be seen in Taylor Swift’s expensive garage. Valued at $126,00, the 911 boasts a powerful 3.7-liter twin-turbo 6-cylinder engine with a powerful 572 HP and 590 lb-ft of torque output. One of the finest cars in the songwriter’s garage, this is one of the best in the Porsche 911 line-up, as per Rerev. A few other well-known names seen with this beauty on wheels are the famous Reggae and Pop artist Rihanna, Mission Impossible hero Tom Cruise and Microsoft founder Bill Gates.

Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG - $156,450

A power-packed automobile and one of the world's most loved SUVs is the Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG. This gem in the Swift garage includes a combination of leisure, luxury, and spacious interiors. Besides, the 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V-8 cylinder engine that produces 577 HP and 627 lb-ft torque makes it an ideal choice for off-roading, according to 21 Motoring.

Mercedes S-Class Maybach S650 - $170,750

Taylor Swift’s Maybach S650, valued at $170,750, epitomizes class, style, and speed. The car can be lowered to 0.7 inches while opting for sport+driving mode, making it a distinct feature. With beautifully detailed interiors and a power engine of a 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V-12 with 621 HP and 738 lb-ft of torque under the hood, this lavish beast is a one-of-a-kind automobile in Swift’s multi-million dollar garage.

Ferrari 458 Italia - $239,350

The Ferrari 458 Italia, priced at $239,350, might be the singer’s most lavish purchase. An absolute beauty on wheels, the Ferrari 458 Italia has a naturally aspirated 4.5-liter V-8 engine with 560 HP and 398 lb-ft of torque. The car’s automated transmission can accelerate from 0 to 60 MPH in around 3.0 seconds, with the utmost pickup speed being 202 MPH. With its sleek design and top-notch interior, the Ferrari might be Taylor’s preferred car for public appearances.

Taylor Swift has demonstrated her well-rounded automobile collection with luxury, speed, power, comfort, and practicality. Swift's versatile garage hosts many cars with different calibers and usage.
