WWE Supershow Results From North Charleston, SC (5/14): Cody Rhodes And More

August 03, 2024
blog

On May 14, WWE held a WWE Supershow event at the North Charleston Coliseum in North Charleston, South Carolina.

In the main event, Cody Rhodes faced Finn Balor in a Street Fight.

The results, courtesy of Wrestling Bodyslam, are as follows:

  • : Bianca Belair (c) defeated Asuka to retain.
  • : Gunther (c) defeated Johnny Gargano to retain.
  • : AJ Styles defeated Austin Theory (c) via disqualification due to a low blow by Theory.
  • : Sami Zayn & Kevin Owens (c) defeated Imperium (Ludwig Kaiser & Giovanni Vinci) and The Street Profits (Montez Ford & Angelo Dawkins)
  • Omos defeated Dolph Ziggler
  • : Rhea Ripley (c) defeated Tegan Nox
  • : Cody Rhodes defeated Finn Balor
