English actress Emily Blunt has a net worth of $16 million. She is a Golden Globe Award-winning actress for the BBC television show “Gideon’s Daughter” in 2007.

Majority of her wealth comes from her long tenure in the show business. She started studying the art of acting at age 16 and attained fame in America after playing a role in the movie “The Devil Wears Prada” in 2006. She has also appeared in The Adjustment Bureau (2011), Looper (2012), and The Young Victoria (2009). Emily debuted in Boudica, a British drama on television in 2003. She has also performed in movies such as The Jane Austen Book Club, Wind Chill, Sunshine Cleaning, Dan in Real Life, The Great buck Howard, The Young Victoria and Curiosity, the short film.

In “Iron Man 2”, the Black Widow’s part was initially offered to her, but later it went to Scarlett Johansson. She also starred in the big budget production The Wolfman in 2010, Gulliver’s Travels, and the 2012 comedy Salmon Fishing in the Yemen. Blunt has been nominated many times – she has four Golden Globes nominations, two for the London Film Critics’ Circle Awards, and one BAFTA. She also provided a voice-over in the television series Angelina Ballerina: The Next Steps. Emily co-presented an award at the 79th Academy Awards with Anne Hathaway.

Born on 23rd February 1983, she is now married to the actor John Krasinski. The Canadian singer Michael Buble shared a three-year relationship with her from 2005 to 2008. Emily started dating Krasinski in November 2008 and got engaged in August 2009. The marriage took place in Como in Italy on 10th July 2010. She is also a member of the Board of Directors for the American Institute for Stuttering. She has been diagnosed with obsessive-compulsive disorder.
