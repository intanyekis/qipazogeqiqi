Karen Trust Grassle Wiki Biography

Karen Trust Grassle (born February 25, 1942) is an American actress, best known for her role as Caroline Ingalls, the wife of Michael Landon's character and the mother of Melissa Gilbert's character, in the NBC television drama series Little House on the Prairie. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
