50 Cents Ex Vivica A Fox Answers Interesting Wrong Questions about Love and Life

May 25, 2024
blog

In a chat with People Magazine, Vivica A. Fox got to answer 25 interesting "Wrong" questions to celebrate Lifetime's 25th "Wrong" movie franchise.

Lifetime has produced 25 movies in the "Wrong" franchise and Vivica A. Fox has been in almost all of the films as either an actress or a producer.

In a chat with People, Fox got to answer 25 interesting questions in celebration of the 25th movie installment for the franchise set to premiere soon. The movie to be premiered is titled "The Wrong Valentine."

Vivica A. Fox visits "Extra" at Universal Studios Hollywood on October 3, 2017. | Photo: Getty Images

The first question wanted to know if she had been at the wrong place at the wrong time, and she replied in the affirmative, talking about how her Mercedes Benz G-Wagon was rear-ended about a month ago.

She then talked about one wrong thing she did often even though it was wrong, and that was going for her third drink even when she should not, as she was a two-drink maximum person. Speaking on a piece of wrong advice she followed, Fox said:

"Listening to my girlfriend and taking a job that ruined my relationship with my boyfriend at the time."

When it came to questions on food, Fox revealed that the right kind of wrong when it came to dessert was chocolate souffle. And for pizza, anchovies were the wrong kind of toppings to be used.

Fox admitted that she doesn't like candy corn, so it was "so wrong" for her. And a wrong snack to binge on when watching a movie was extra spicy hot chips.

Fox has established that she was open to looking for love despite her busy schedule.

When asked if she had ever worn the wrong shoes, Fox narrated how she went to a stadium concert wearing heels, and she had to do a lot of walking. It turned out to be a bad idea for her after all.

She added that she rocked the wrong hair and makeup back in the '80s and a wrong outfit to the premiere of the movie "Batman and Robin," which starred George Clooney. On a splurge that is the right kind of wrong, Fox answered saying:

"Diamonds"

Speaking on a time she crushed on the wrong guy, Fox said she discovered the man was married, and the moment she found out, he instantly became unavailable in her eyes.

Fox also touched on how she sometimes calls people by their wrong name because she was terrible with names and how she often pretended to be wrong to avoid an argument with her friends.

Aside from some of the "Wrong" questions, the former "Empire" actress once revealed how she had a terrible fear of heights and would never miss the opportunity to visit Montego Bay, Jamaica, and Paris.

Fox has established that she was open to looking for love despite her busy schedule, and even though she met someone during the quarantine, things were not looking so great. Hopefully, Fox does not meet the wrong man.
