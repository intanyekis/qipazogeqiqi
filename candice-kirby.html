Candice Kirby is an American social media star, best known for her collaborative YouTube channel with her best friend, Quincie Pullen, named “QuincieandCandice”, which has amassed more than 700k subscribers. The channel was created on September 20, 2013, but they didn’t publish their 1st video, titled “Candice’s Spring Outfits!! (Winter into spring)” until February 2014. They used it as a medium to showcase their fashion taste and do challenges such as dressing as Kylie Jenner and Kendall Jenner for a week and give beauty and lifestyle advice.

Quincie and she also created regular comedy skits, vlogs, pranks, as well as DIY creative tips, tricks, and ideas regarding home decoration, gift ideas for various holidays and occasions, as well as reviewed numerous trending products honestly. Candice used her personal YouTube channel, simply titled “Candice Kirby” to make herself more accessible and in touch with an audience of more than 20k subscribers. She has also amassed a fanbase online with more than 80k followers on Instagram and more than 30k followers on Twitter.
