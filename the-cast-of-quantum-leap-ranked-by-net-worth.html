The Cast Of Quantum Leap Ranked By Net Worth

May 18, 2024
blog

The NBC show Quantum Leap first premiered in 1989 and ran for four years, following a scientist that involuntarily leaps through time during experimentation with time travel. In 2022, NBC traveled back in time themselves, giving Quantum Leap a reboot with a similar premise to the original.

The 2022 series of Quantum Leap features Raymond Lee as Dr. Ben Song. Like the scientist from the original series, Ben travels through time via the same mechanism — the Project Accelerator. However in this series, Ben travels voluntarily, without telling any of the other team members working on the project alongside him. They are then tasked with bringing Ben home safely, all while trying to uncover why he leapt in the first place.

The show features a star-studded cast, and many fans may recognize the cast of Quantum Leap from other projects. Here's more on the background of each major cast member, with each ranked in order of net worth.

8 Raymond Lee - $5 Million

Raymond Lee takes on the role of the show's lead, Dr. Ben Song. Over the course of his career so far, Lee has amassed a net worth of $5 million. He's held minor roles in shows such as Scandal, Modern Family, and It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia. He's also held more notable roles in projects such as Mozart in the Jungle,Here and Now, and Kevin Can F**K Himself.

7 Caitlin Bassett - $5 Million

Caitlin Bassett plays Addison Augustine, Ben's fiancé and the team member in charge of entering into the simulator to help Ben as he makes his way through his leaps.

Bassett actually just made her acting debut, and before her career in Hollywood, she spent seven years in the U.S. Army Intelligence and the National Security Agency. After that, she joined the Stellar Adler Conservatory full-time before joining the Disney Discovers Talent Showcase cast in 2020. In addition to Quantum Leap, she's also held a role as Mrs. Hamill in the short film V is for Vodka (2020) and is one of the panel members on the show The Back Page Women's Week Special, which looks to celebrate women in sport.

6 Ernie Hudson - $5 Million

Ernie Hudson takes on the role of Herbert "Magic" Williams in Quantum Leap. Hudson is the lead of the Quantum Leap project team and a Vietnam War veteran that Sam, from the original series, leapt into during his time in the Accelerator.

Hudson has held a number of roles that have contributed to his $5 million net worth. Most notably, he played Winston Zeddemore in the Ghostbusters series, and with so many renditions of the series, a lot of work went into the making of the Ghostbuster films. He's also starred in the movie Miss Congeniality (2000) and the Netflix series Grace and Frankie.

5 Georgina Reilly - $5 Million

Georgina Reilly plays Janis Calavicci, who is the daughter of Al Calavicci from the original Quantum Leap series. In the 2022 reboot, Janis is the only character that Ben told about why he needed to leap in the first place.

In addition to Quantum Leap, Reilly's most notable role is portraying Dr. Emily Grace on the Canadian drama series Murdoch Mysteries. She's also held roles in a few movies, including playing Caroline in This Movie is Broken (2010) and Piper in The Baker and the Beauty (2020).

4 Mason Alexander Park - $4-6 Million

Mason Alexander Park takes on the role of Ian Wright, the chief architect of the project team that specializes in artificial intelligence. Before Quantum Leap, Park held notable roles in the anime Cowboy Bebop and Neil Gaiman's The Sandman, which helped them amass their $4-6 million net worth. Park's first big break as an actor was in a 2011 episode of iCarly called "iLove You." They also took part in theater productions, playing Hedwig in Hedwig and the Angry Inch on the Broadway National Tour.

3 Walter Perez - $1.5 Million

Walter Perez plays Richard Martinez, who is dubbed as 'leaper X' in the series. In addition to Quantum Leap, Perez has also held roles as Victor Tavares in Fame (2009), Jason in Line of Duty (2013), and Bobby "Bull" Reyes in Friday Night Lights. He's also had guest appearances in shows such as Grey's Anatomy, CSI: Miami, and Castle.

2 Susan Doil - $1 Million

Susan Doil takes on the role of Beth Calavicci, who is the wife of Al Calavicci from the original series. She appeared in only two episodes of the original Quantum Leap, but reprised the role in the reboot.

In addition to the Quantum Leap shows, Doil is known for her role of Emmy Borden in Days of Our Lives, which had some iconic moments over the soap opera's 56-year history, including a time jump of its own. In total, Doil has had supporting roles in over 40 series, including guest-starring in shows such as Baywatch and Seinfeld.

1 Nanrisa Lee - Nearly $1 Million

Nanrisa Lee plays Jenn Chu in the show, the Quantum Leap head of security, who makes up the fifth and final member of the project team.

Quantum Leap is actually Lee's first time holding a role as a series regular. Before portraying Jenn, Lee was a guest-star in various shows, including NCIS: Los Angeles, American Horror Story, and Grey's Anatomy. She's also held roles in movies like Hansel vs Gretel (2015), The Aliens (2017), and First Love (2022).
